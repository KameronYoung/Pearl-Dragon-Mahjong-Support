# Privacy Policy for Pearl Dragon Mahjong

_Last updated: April 15, 2026_

Pearl Dragon Mahjong does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game data, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device and are never shared with third parties.

## Data Collection: 
None. The app does not use analytics, tracking, advertising, or any network services.

## Third-Party Services: 
None. The app does not integrate with any third-party SDKs or services.

## Children's Privacy: 
The app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

If this policy changes, updates will be posted here.
